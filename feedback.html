About Us

Welcome to Typing Boost! Our mission is to enhance your typing skills with the best tools available. Whether you're a beginner looking to improve your speed or an experienced typist aiming for perfection, we have something for everyone.


At Typing Boost, we offer a variety of features designed to make your typing practice engaging and effective:


Word Counter: Keep track of your progress and set goals with our easy-to-use word counter. Monitor your output and watch your skills grow!


Typing Tests: Challenge yourself with our dynamic typing tests, designed to assess your speed and accuracy. Compete against yourself or share your scores with friends!


Tricky Keys: Tackle the toughest keys with our targeted exercises that focus on tricky letters and combinations, helping you build confidence and improve your overall performance.


Join us at Typing Boost and take your typing skills to new heights! Let's type smarter, not harder
